Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about space and our website.

How far is the Moon from Earth?

The Moon is approximately 384,400 kilometers (238,855 miles) away from Earth on average.

How long does it take to travel to Mars?

With current technology, it takes about 6-9 months to travel from Earth to Mars, depending on the positions of both planets.

Can I use your images for my school project?

Yes! Our placeholder images and content are free to use for educational purposes. Please credit Space Explorer in your project.

How do I learn more about HTML and CSS?
