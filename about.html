Footer Design Tips

Good footers help users navigate and find important information.

Footer Best Practices

  • Keep it simple: Don't put too much information
  • Use columns: Organize content in sections
  • Include links: Help users navigate your site
  • Add contact info: Make it easy to reach you
  • Copyright notice: Protect your content

Footer HTML Structure

<footer>
  <div class="footer-content">
    <div class="footer-section">
      <h3>Contact</h3>
      <p>Your address here</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</footer>

Space Facts

The Moon

The Moon is about 384,400 kilometers away from Earth. It takes about 3 days to travel there.

Mars

Mars is called the Red Planet because of iron oxide on its surface. A day on Mars is about 24 hours and 37 minutes.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. It has at least 95 moons, including four large ones discovered by Galileo.
